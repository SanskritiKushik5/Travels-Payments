ABOUT

There's something unique about the intersection of Travel & Payments sectors (or services). Both are growing faster than any other and unless another COVID strikes, remain evergreen businesses. The space of Travel & Payments is a union of everything related to Travel & Payments explaining in lay-Sapiens terms, the nuances of each.

The goal of Travel & Payments is to democratize the Payments knowledge while sharing travel stories to unique places of the world (or the solar system once it becomes affordable)

VISHAL MEHTA

The founder of this Travel & Payments space, Vishal Mehta, has spent majority of his career in the Payments industry, even before the fintech term became omnipresent. All this, while traveling to 49 Countries across all 7 continents of the world. He has worked for Playspan (acquired by VISA) & Emergent Payments on the Payment Service Provider end followed by an intense traveling role at Expedia & now at Microsoft, managing all partnerships with external payments providers. Vishal is also the chair person of the merchant payments' industry body - Merchant Advisory Group (MAG)'s APAC chapter.

Vishal aims to share his travel stories across the 7 continents and help refine customized itineraries for those who don't have time to do it themselves. Vishal also strongly believes in democratization of Payments knowledge for the merchants and aims to make life easier for them. by making Payments, usually the biggest cost head after Infrastructure & Salaries for consumer companies, one less thing to worry about.

He can be reached at vishal@travelandpayments.com
