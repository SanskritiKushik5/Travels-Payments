TO TRAVEL IS TO LIVE

Traveling as a habit has been subconsciously imbibed by my parents as every alternate school vacation meant a trip to some part of India or the other. By the time I made my first international trip as an exchange student to Sweden in 2010, my parents had taken us traveling to 19 states & 3 UTs of India. For a middle-class family, I realized later, this was an achievement and I appreciate this emphasis on travel by my parents even more as time progresses while continuing to explore more countries.

As of September 2021, I have been to 49 countries (at least one in each continent) in the last 10 years. Within India, this figure has reached 20 states & 5 UTs, with a plan in place to travel to two more states in the next few months.

The whole idea of the "Travel" space as part of "Travel & Payments" is to offer insights into various aspects of traveling to some of the unique places around the world through my and other travelers' lenses.

More importantly, it is also to offer an "Itinerary as a service" for those souls who don't have enough time to make customized plans for an upcoming trip. This service offering isn't bread and butter for us, but were passionate about getting the best out of every trip and helping fellow travelers do the same through our experiences globally.

While we're not aiming to be a travel agency to book travel through, we certainly aim to be the first stop for those travel journeys which would be etched in memories for rest of our lifetimes.

-- Vishal Mehta
