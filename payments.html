Entering the Payments space was a chance than a choice, a lucky break which I secretly thank each atom and molecule in the universe for making it happen. In the last 10 years of Payments career, I've worked at Payment Service Providers (Playspan & Emergent Payments) and merchants (Expedia) while I currently manage Payments partnerships at Microsoft (as a merchant).

I am very passionate about payments, particularly how it impacts the merchants in the digital space. This passion led me to take up the Chairperson role for the APAC chapter of MAG (Merchant Advisory Group) and now has also motivated me to start the "Payments" space at Travel & Payments.

There is a saturation of Fintech related content and new keeps coming up every day, but rarely, if any, on how to optimize the current state of payments affairs in your organization. The goal of this Payments space is to educate the merchants, whether they're just joining the ecommerce bandwagon or onboarding folks from non-Payments backgrounds through an EXPLAINER series - on how Payments works, especially from their and consumer's viewpoints. We will also achieve this by writing opinion pieces from perspective of Payments experts on various aspects impacting merchants.

-- Vishal Mehta
